Flutter release surface requirements
- Flutter is the primary SMB launch client.
- No production navigation path may end in a placeholder or dead-end action.
- Shipped claims are limited to the in-scope modules defined in docs/SMB_EDITION_SCOPE.md.
- Flutter-called endpoints must remain present in go_backend_rmt/openapi.yaml and tools/api_parity_report.md must stay clean.
- Offline/outbox claims are limited to flows already documented and UAT-covered.
- Production builds must supply a real API_BASE_URL rather than relying on the localhost fallback.

Primary functional modules for the Flutter surface
- auth and bootstrap
- dashboard and location-aware navigation
- POS and sales
- purchases and suppliers
- inventory
- customers and collections
- accounting essentials
- reports
- HR core
- workflow/notifications in limited scope
- admin/settings and bulk import/export

Out-of-scope launch claims
- enterprise identity and governance
- multi-entity finance
- bank reconciliation completeness
- browser-first office parity
